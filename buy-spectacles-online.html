Mastering Pupillary Distance (PD) and Other Critical Measurements

Beyond your prescription, accurate physical measurements of your face and existing frames are vital for comfort and optimal vision. The most frequently overlooked, yet incredibly important, measurement is your pupillary distance.

Why PD Matters:

Your pupillary distance (PD) is the measurement in millimeters between the centers of your pupils. If the optical centers of your new **spectacles online** don't align with your pupils, you might experience blurry vision, eye strain, or headaches, even if the prescription power is correct.
