After every eye exam, it's vital to always secure a **hard copy and a digital version of your prescription**. Decoding lens types for affordable glasses

#### Decoding lens types for affordable glasses

Understanding the different **types of lenses for affordable glasses** is key to making an informed decision that balances vision correction with cost. Each type serves a specific purpose, catering to various visual needs and preferences.

Single-vision lenses are the most common and often the most affordable option. They correct for either near or far vision only, making them ideal for individuals who need assistance with reading or driving, but not both simultaneously. These are perfect for basic `economical spectacles`.

For those requiring both near and distance correction, **bifocal lenses** offer a practical solution. These lenses feature a distinct, visible line separating the two corrective areas. While functional, the line can sometimes be a visual adjustment for wearers.

**Trifocal lenses** take it a step further, providing distance, intermediate, and near vision correction. They are particularly useful for individuals over 50 who need support for tasks at arm's length, such as computer work, in addition to reading and driving.

However, for a seamless visual experience, **progressive lenses** are often preferred. These lenses offer a smooth transition between different focal areas without visible lines, providing a more natural and aesthetically pleasing look.
