Decoding Your Pupillary Distance (PD): The Crucial Measurement

While often overlooked, your pupillary distance (PD) is arguably the most vital measurement when ordering **value prescription eyewear** online. It dictates where the optical center of your lenses should be placed.

* **Importance:** **Pupillary distance for online glasses** is absolutely vital for accurate lens centering. An incorrect PD can lead to blurry vision, eye strain, headaches, or even double vision, even if your prescription numbers are correct. It ensures your eyes are looking through the optimal part of the lens.

* **Acquisition:** Always insist on getting your PD from your optometrist during your eye exam. If they don't provide it, you can measure it yourself using various online guides or dedicated apps provided by most online stores. These tools often use a credit card or a ruler and your phone's camera to help you get a reasonably accurate measurement. Many online stores, like EyeBuyDirect, provide clear instructions and tools to help you measure your PD accurately.
